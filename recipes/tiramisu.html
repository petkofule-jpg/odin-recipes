Tiramisu

A classic Italian dessert made with coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cream, and cocoa powder.

Ingredients

  • 250 g mascarpone cheese
  • 3 eggs
  • 100 g sugar
  • 200 g ladyfingers
  • 200 ml strong coffee
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Brew the coffee and let it cool.
  2. Separate egg whites and yolks, whip yolks with sugar, then add mascarpone.
  3. Beat the egg whites and fold into mascarpone mixture.
  4. Dip ladyfingers in coffee and layer with cream mixture.
  5. Chill in fridge for at least 4 hours and dust with cocoa before serving.
