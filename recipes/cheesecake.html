Cheesecake

A creamy and rich dessert with a buttery biscuit base, perfect with fruit toppings.

Ingredients

  • 200 g digestive biscuits
  • 100 g butter, melted
  • 600 g cream cheese
  • 150 g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 200 ml sour cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Crush the biscuits and mix with melted butter. Press into a cake tin base.
  2. Beat cream cheese with sugar, then add eggs one at a time, mixing well.
  3. Stir in sour cream and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour mixture over the biscuit base and bake at 160°C for 50–60 minutes.
  5. Let it cool, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
