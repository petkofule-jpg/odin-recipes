Crème Brûlée

A French classic with silky vanilla custard topped with a crunchy caramelized sugar crust.

Ingredients

  • 500 ml heavy cream
  • 5 egg yolks
  • 100 g sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Extra sugar for caramel topping

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C.
  2. Heat cream with vanilla until hot but not boiling.
  3. Whisk egg yolks with sugar, then slowly add warm cream while whisking.
  4. Pour into ramekins and bake in a water bath for about 40 minutes until set.
  5. Chill, then sprinkle sugar on top and caramelize with a torch before serving.
